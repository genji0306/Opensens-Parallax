# Design System Strategy: The Precision Editorial

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
This design system is built for the high-stakes world of scientific research. We are moving away from the generic "SaaS dashboard" look toward a **"Precision Editorial"** North Star. 

Scientific data shouldn't just be legible; it should be authoritative. This system treats UI like a high-end scientific journal—using intentional asymmetry, vast white space, and a sophisticated layering of information to manage high data density without clutter. We break the "template" feel by layering data on top of semi-transparent surfaces, utilizing monospaced accents for technical integrity, and employing a depth model that mimics physical laboratory slides.

## 2. Colors & Surface Logic
To maintain a high-end feel, we rely on **Tonal Contrast** rather than structural lines.
