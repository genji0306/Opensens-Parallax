# Exploration Topic

Hydrolysis of Tetra-butyl Titanate (TBT) in Water-Friendly Solvent System as Binder for Zinc Flake Coating Process

## Goal
Explore the chemistry, formulation logic, likely hydrolysis/condensation behavior, process constraints, and coating-process implications for using TBT in a water-friendly solvent system as a binder precursor for zinc flake coatings.

## Key Questions
- How can TBT be hydrolyzed in a partially aqueous solvent system without immediate uncontrolled gelation?
- What co-solvents or chelating modifiers are likely needed?
- What hydrolysis-condensation window is compatible with zinc flake coating processing?
- What are the likely risks for pot life, adhesion, cure profile, and corrosion resistance?
